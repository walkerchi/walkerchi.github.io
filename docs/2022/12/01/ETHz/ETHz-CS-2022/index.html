  2. Increase this minimal cable to a useful capability

2.3 Climber

  1. total mass: 619kg(288kg cable)
  2. the cable deployed by first climber will be shorter and stronger(91,000km < 117,00km, 9.7kg > 8kg)
  3. first 207 climbers widen the edge of the initial ribbon to 30cm at 200km/h(reduce catastrophic and meteor damage). Once it reaches 30cm width, then thicken the ribbon.
  4. expandable design as it become larger each climb
  5. stuck: low altitude-> retrieve, high altitude->release
  6. if power-kilogram ratio > 40%(70kW for 113W/kg). strengthen cable to 20,000kg in 1.7 years, 1000,000kg in 3.7years. And also reduce cable damage(could reach 47.84 kw/kg in 2022, 3000kW 62.7kg)
